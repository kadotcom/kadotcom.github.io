SCP-682 in SCP:SL

This video is from May 23th, 2022 and is of an old test version.

SCP-682, also known as Impossible-To-Destroy/Immortal Reptile, is an keter-level SCP that has been described as 'large, vaguely reptile-like creature of unknown origin. It appears to be extremely intelligent, and was observed to engage in complex communication with SCP-079 during their limited time of exposure. SCP-682 appears to have a hatred of all life.'

It's a pretty popular SCP but it isn't in SCP:SL. So this plugin adds SCP-682 (although as of right now, it can only be spawned with a command in Remote Admin)
